# Dashboard User Guide

## Overview
This document explains how to access, navigate, and interpret the Power BI dashboard built using the analytics outputs from the E-Commerce Data Pipeline.

The dashboard enables business users to explore sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and geographic insights interactively.

---

## Accessing the Dashboard

### Power BI
- File Location: `dashboards/powerbi/ecommerce_analytics.pbix`
- Tool Required: **Power BI Desktop (Free Version)**
- Data Source: PostgreSQL Analytics Tables / Generated CSV Analytics

---

## Dashboard Pages

---

### Page 1: Executive Summary
**Purpose:**  
Provides a high-level overview of overall business performance for quick decision-making.

**Key Metrics (Cards):**
- Total Revenue
- Total Transactions
- Average Order Value
- Profit Margin

**Visualizations:**
- Line chart showing monthly revenue trend
- Bar chart of top-performing product categories
- Donut chart for payment method distribution
- Map visual showing geographic sales distribution

**Business Questions Answered:**
- How is the business performing overall?
- Are revenues increasing or declining?
- Which categories and payment methods dominate sales?

---

### Page 2: Product Analysis
**Purpose:**  
Analyzes product-level performance to identify top and underperforming items.

**Key Visuals:**
- Top 10 products by revenue
- Category-wise revenue and units sold
- Profit margin comparison across categories

**Business Questions Answered:**
- Which products generate the most revenue?
- Which categories are most profitable?
- Are certain products underperforming?

---

### Page 3: Customer Insights
**Purpose:**  
Examines customer behavior and contribution patterns.

**Key Visuals:**
- Customer growth over time
- Revenue contribution by customer segments
- Repeat vs one-time customer analysis

**Business Questions Answered:**
- Who are the most valuable customers?
- Is customer retention improving?
- How much revenue comes from repeat customers?

---

### Page 4: Geographic & Time-Based Trends
**Purpose:**  
Analyzes regional and temporal sales trends.

**Key Visuals:**
- State-wise revenue distribution
- Day-wise sales trends (weekday vs weekend)
- Seasonal or monthly performance patterns

**Business Questions Answered:**
- Which regions generate the highest revenue?
- Do weekends perform better than weekdays?
- Are there seasonal sales patterns?

---

## Filters & Interactivity
The dashboard supports interactive filtering to allow deeper analysis:

**Available Filters (Slicers):**
- Date range
- Product category
- Customer state
- Payment method

**Usage:**
- Filters apply across all visuals on the page
- Enables drill-down into specific segments or periods

---

## Refreshing the Dashboard Data
To update the dashboard with the latest data:

1. Run the complete ETL pipeline:
   ```bash
   python scripts/pipeline_orchestrator.py
2. Open Power BI Desktop

3. Click Refresh

4. Updated insights will be reflected in all visuals
